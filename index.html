Rooted in Surf Culture

Born from the laid-back beaches of California in 1995, Wave Kine emerged from a deep love for surf culture and the desire to create clothing that reflects the authentic spirit of ocean living. We're more than just a brand—we're a lifestyle.

Every piece we create is inspired by the endless summer, the thrill of dawn patrol, and the timeless connection between surfers and the sea. Our designs blend classic surf heritage with contemporary style, creating apparel that looks good both in the water and on the street.

From organic cotton tees to performance boardshorts, sustainable hoodies to vintage-inspired accessories—we craft each item with the environment and surf community in mind.
